Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Startup Companion

Can I use this app even if I don't have prior experience in law or business?

Absolutely! Each tool, template, and explainer is crafted for beginners.

Will this app guide me through government grants or schemes available for new businesses?

Yes, the Company Navigator matches relevant programs (Startup India, MSME) and demystifies eligibility.

What happens if I have a compliance question the AI can't solve?

The integrated Legal Desk connects you to human experts, on-demand.

Who should use Startup Companion?

First-time service business founders in India or the U.S. needing straightforward, stepwise startup guidance.

Does the app fill out forms for me?

No; it only guides you through what forms are needed and where/who to contact for completion.

Can I use this for product startups?

Startup Companion is optimized for service businesses but many steps apply to product-based startups too.

Is this free?

Basic guidance is always free, with optional paid expert consults for complex cases.

Does this help with both India and U.S. requirements?

Yes; all guidance is tailored for both jurisdictions with dedicated compliance and registration resource links.
